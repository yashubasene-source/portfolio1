Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before hiring me.

What is your typical turnaround time?

For short reels under 5 minutes, I deliver in 24–48 hours. For longer projects like documentaries, expect 5–7 working days. Rush delivery is available at extra charge.

How do I send you my raw footage?

Share via Google Drive, WeTransfer, or Dropbox. For very large files I can provide a private upload link. WhatsApp me first to coordinate file transfer.

How many revisions do you offer?

All packages include 2 revision rounds by default. Unlimited revisions are available as an add-on for ₹800. I prefer getting the brief right from the start.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Absolutely! I work with global clients and accept PayPal and Wise for international payments. Communication is smooth on WhatsApp or email.

What software do you use?

Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve for color grading, Adobe Audition for audio, Photoshop & Illustrator for graphics, and Figma for UI work.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, bank transfer, PayPal, Wise. Structure: 50% advance before work starts, 50% on delivery. Full payment upfront for orders under ₹1,000.

Do you offer monthly retainer packages?

Yes! Monthly packages with priority slots, dedicated delivery time, and discounted rates. Ideal for agencies and YouTube channels needing consistent content output.

Can you handle confidential or NDA work?

Absolutely. I sign NDAs for political campaigns, corporate projects, and sensitive content. Client confidentiality is something I take very seriously.
